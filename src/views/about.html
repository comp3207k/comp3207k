What is CinemaBuddy?

CinemaBuddy is a brand new web based application aimed bringing like-minded movie fans together, because afterall the movies are better with friends.

CinemaBuddy is an application developed by Team K is designed as a social web based application aimed at bringing together like minded movie-fans enabling them to enjoy the cinema and movies together. CinemaBuddy displays allows users to search screenings of every film being shown at any Cineworld Cinema nationwide complimented with a plot synopsis and poster (IMDB) and a rotten tomatoes rating, as well as the age certificate from the BBFC (British Board of Film Classification).

CinemaBuddy provides a platform that enables its users to select both current and upcoming movie releases, and then get “matched” with other users who want to see the same movie. The application also factors in users locations based on the cinemasthey are willing to travel too and the times in which they are available, when suggesting screenings and “cinemaBuddies” to be matched with.
